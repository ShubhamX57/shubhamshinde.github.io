About

I am a Scientific Software Engineer with 3+ years of industry experience, currently pursuing an MSc in Scientific Computing with Data Science at the University of Bristol — supported by the prestigious SARTHI Maharashtra Government Foreign Scholarship.

My work lives at the intersection of high-performance computing and research software engineering. I have built production data pipelines handling 20 GB+ daily sensor streams in HPC, developed Bayesian MCMC analysis tools for neutron scattering experiments, and shipped full-stack scientific services from bare FastAPI backend to React monitoring dashboards.

I am particularly drawn to problems where software craftsmanship meets scientific rigour — designing systems that are not just functional, but reproducible, testable, and long-lived enough to serve a research community.
